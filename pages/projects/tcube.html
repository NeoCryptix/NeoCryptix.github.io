What is Ch2?

TransCube Chapter 2, abbreviated as Ch2, is a mod and remix of Jeroen Verfallie's winning GGO13 game Transcube. It takes the wonderful, charming puzzle platformer genre and adds a transformative twist, making for a fun web game with great replayability. Our mod adds difficulty to the original 22 levels, by modifying the solutions and abilities the player is given. We also redesigned the color pallette, to create a new atmosphere fitting of the mod. New levels are currently in development, and you can play the most recent updates with the link below.
